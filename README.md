# JCROBOT

Our team adheres to the corporate philosophy of "Focus, Excellence, Innovation, and Speed." We are a high‑tech enterprise dedicated to the mobile robot chassis sector, benchmarking ourselves against advanced international technologies. The company has been awarded the Gold Award in the "Internet+" National College Students Entrepreneurship Competition and has been selected for the "New Enterprise List" of the China Association for Science and Technology's "Innovation China" initiative.

Our products are primarily deployed in scenarios such as emergency rescue, military applications, delivery logistics, inspection, security, and special‑environment operations. To date, we have developed 30 mature chassis models across tracked, wall‑climbing, and wheeled platforms, along with over 10 core technologies, resulting in more than 60 patents. We have supplied over 2,500 tracked and wheeled robot chassis to more than 700 development teams worldwide, significantly lowering the barriers to mobile robotics innovation.

With our programmable drive‑by‑wire chassis and a rich ecosystem of peripheral components, we enable users to accelerate the R&D process of mobile robots, ensuring that global robotics innovations are brought to life efficiently and effectively.
